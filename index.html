1. Song Architecture

Verse: The "Who, What, Where." Keep details specific here.

Chorus: The "Big Idea." Should be catchy and easy to remember.

Bridge: The "Twist." Change the melody or the emotional angle.

2. Storytelling (The Arc)

  • Verse 1: Introduce the problem or setting.
  • Verse 2: Develop the tension or add a new character.
  • Verse 3: Resolution or final reflection.

3. Advanced Rhyming

  • Perfect Rhyme: Cat / Hat (Identical ending sounds).
  • Slant Rhyme: Storm / Alone (Similar sounds, but not exact). Great for modern vibes.
  • Internal Rhyme: Rhyming words within the same line.

4. Meter & Flow

Use the Syl Counter on your blocks! Try to keep Verse lines within 1-2 syllables of each other to maintain a natural "pocket" or rhythm.

5. Sensory Writing

Don't just say "I'm sad." Describe a cold room, a broken glass, or a grey sky. Show, don't tell.
